FFmpeg (bundled ffmpeg.exe)
===========================

Some Windows builds of Improve ImgSLI include ffmpeg.exe for video export.
FFmpeg is licensed under the GNU General Public License v2.0 or later
(GPL-2.0-or-later) and/or GNU Lesser General Public License v2.1 or later
(LGPL-2.1-or-later), depending on build configuration.

Source code and license information:
  https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html

The bundled binary is typically obtained from community builds such as:
  https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds

You may replace ffmpeg.exe with another compatible build if required by your
distribution policy. Keep the executable name ffmpeg.exe in the application
directory or update your PATH accordingly.
