## Hotkeys

This page is a short cheat sheet. Detailed behavior is described in the thematic pages.

### Canvas {#canvas}
- `Ctrl + mouse wheel` — zoom.
- `Middle Mouse Button` above `100%` zoom — pan.
- `Space + Left Mouse Button` — quick preview of Image 1.
- `Space + Right Mouse Button` — quick preview of Image 2.
- `Space + Shift + Left/Right Mouse Button` — force a side in combined magnifier preview.

### Magnifier {#magnifier}
- `WASD` — move the view or the frozen magnifier.
- `QE` — change spacing between magnifier halves.

### Files And Saving {#files-and-saving}
- `Ctrl+V` — paste an image from the clipboard.
- `Ctrl+S` — quick save with the last export settings.
- `Ctrl+Shift+S` — open the export dialog.

### Video Editor {#video-editor}
- `Spacebar` — play / pause.
- `Ctrl+Z` — undo.
- `Ctrl+Y` or `Ctrl+Shift+Z` — redo.
- `Delete` / `Backspace` — remove the selected timeline range.

### Paste Overlay {#paste-overlay}
- `Arrow keys` or `WASD` — choose paste direction.
- `Esc` — cancel paste.
